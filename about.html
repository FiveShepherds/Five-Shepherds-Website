ABOUT US

Engineering Better Food Processing Solutions

Founded in 2024, Five Shepherds Corporation is committed to helping farmers, entrepreneurs, cooperatives, research institutions, and food processors create premium value-added food products through innovative vacuum frying technology.


Our mission is to manufacture durable, efficient, and reliable food processing equipment that increases profitability while preserving the natural quality of agricultural products.


Located in Sitio Macopa, Brgy. Kan-ipa, Baybay City, Leyte, we proudly serve clients across the Philippines by providing quality machinery, installation, training, and after-sales support.

Mission

Deliver innovative food processing solutions that empower agricultural industries and improve livelihoods.

Vision

To become one of the Philippines' leading manufacturers of food processing equipment.
